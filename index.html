What is ABC?
Athens Burn Consortium (ABC) is a group of people who are interested in learning and practicing the art of fire performance, such as fire spinning, fire dancing, fire breathing, etc. Each member has his/her own preferred apparatus in which they douse in fuel and ignite. The individuals in this group have the most amazing, colorful, creative, extraverted, introverted, and all together unique personalities of anyone I've met. And each person's performance style is as unique as their personality. Some prefer to dance with fire. Some take a more technical approach, emphasizing precision in each movement. Some just spark it up and see what happens, letting the mood of the moment guide their motion. But the one thing we all have in common is the way we feel when we are in the zone. The sound of the roaring flames as they orbit around you, the balance of respect and control you must maintain to avoid being burned, and the feeling of the rest of the world disappearing make fire performance one of the most exciting things you can do with just an object and an element.
